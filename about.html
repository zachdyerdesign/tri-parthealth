My Story

What is your story? Good question?

Your story is filled with signs of God’s hand at work—both seen and unseen.

Make no mistake, He has been there from the time you were born—all along.

Wade will coach you through a four-hour intensive survey of your life. Through the day, Wade will be uncovering new insights into your life. He will give you your own My Story Journal complete with a detailed life compilation; the clear marks of God hidden in your childhood, your school days, your relationships, your work, and your desires. These gems are clues that when pinpointed, propel you toward your New Future. This will help change your story!

Here are a few foundational scriptures.

God created you and He knows your Story intimately—

For you created my inmost being; you knit me together in my mother's womb. (Psalm 139:13)

God wrote the days of your life in His book in advance—

All the days ordained for me were written in your book before one of them came to be. (Psalm 139:16)

God created the time of your birth and the exact places where you would live—

He determined the times set for them and the exact places where they should live. (Acts 17:26)

About Wade

Wade is a Certified Transformational Coach and Coach Trainer whose years of experience helps guide searching people who are looking for a better life.

His insights consistently unlock heart desires and shift people into creative planning and action.

Those who Wade has coached through change, get where they want to go. He helps others with visions of a new future. He has the unique ability to connect dreams and desires to action. All attest to Wade’s artful impact in personal transformation.

His experience includes starting Vineyard Christian Fellowship of Independence with John Wimber, and serving on the Board of Directors of Bridge Ministries International with John Paul Jackson.

Wade has a passion for dream interpretation, and enjoys coaching others in their pursuit of hearing God.

Wade is married to Jan Hankins. They have four adult children. Wade and Jan live in Lee’s Summit, Missouri, at Lake Jacomo.
